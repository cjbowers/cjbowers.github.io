TYPOGRAPHIC DIALOGUES

Barcelona is a nexus of communication. For centuries it has been a crossroads of civilizations, bringing together different cultures and languages in a rich dialogue of language, form, and ideas. Merging a variety of typefaces to form a unique communication structure speaks to the nature of typography and type design today.

MODERN DRIFT

The emergence of mobile maps has made getting lost today nearly impossible. Smart phones encourage users to become passengers, effectively turning off their critical observations. The result is a severe obstruction of our ability to generate accurate and expansive mental maps. My presentation, Modern Drift, explores the effects of releasing our desires and aspirations upon the city through aimless exploration.
