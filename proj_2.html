USCG Ice Breakers

About

If you thought shoveling out your car was difficult this past winter, try to imagine what clearing rivers of ice was like for the Coast Guard. Every year, icebreakers ensure fuel deliveries and other maritime traffic by breaking ice to clear essential routes. Most people don't realize that they rely on the success of the Coast Guard during harsh winter conditions.

After meeting with D1 icebreakers and discussing their needs, I learned that awareness and advocacy was a priority. In response, I designed an educational website to shine a light on the importance of their work. The content is divided into three narrative components.
