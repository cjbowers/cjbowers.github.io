PROFILE WORK

Bound by Ink

Freedom and security is a perpetual debate in all developed societies. How do we determine where the line of the law falls in the massive grey area of this issue? During the red scare, Loyalty Oaths were inacted in several Universities. These oaths were a clear violation of the second am- mendment, and many faculty refused to sign the documents. To spread awareness and reflect on this issue, I created a dos-a-doe artist book. The book divides those who oppose and support the oaths. The voice of the supporters is sup- pressed by an elastic band meant as a continuation of the signature line. Peer pressure and rhetoric drove this issue, and I explored the rela- tionship of a book as a medium for expression.
